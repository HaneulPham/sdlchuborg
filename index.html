Birds of the Month

Cardinal

The northern cardinal is one of the most recognized birds in North America. Males are bright red, and females are a warm reddish brown.

Blue Jay

Blue Jays are known for their striking blue plumage and loud calls. They are highly intelligent birds with complex social systems.

American Goldfinch

These small, bright yellow birds are often found among fields and gardens. They have a bouncy flight and can be seen clinging to seed feeders.

Did you know?

Certain bird species migrate thousands of miles every year, often traveling the same path each migration season.
